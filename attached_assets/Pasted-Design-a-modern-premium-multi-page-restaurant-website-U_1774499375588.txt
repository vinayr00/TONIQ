Design a modern, premium multi-page restaurant website UI in Figma for **"Tonique Restaurant & Bar"**.

### 🎨 Design Style

* Theme: Dark, elegant, nightlife vibe
* Colors:

  * Background: Deep black (#0f0f0f)
  * Accent: Gold / Orange (#f59e0b)
  * Text: White / light gray
* Style: Luxury + party atmosphere (bar + restaurant)
* Use soft shadows, glassmorphism, and subtle glow effects

---

### 📱 Responsiveness

Design for:

* Desktop (1440px)
* Tablet (768px)
* Mobile (375px)

---

### 🧱 Pages to Design

#### 1. Home Page

Sections:

* Hero section (large background image with overlay text)

  * Heading: “Craft Cocktails. Unforgettable Nights.”
  * Buttons: “Reserve Table”, “View Menu”
* Highlights (Cocktails, Ambience, Party vibe)
* Featured Menu preview (cards with images, no prices)
* Gallery preview (grid layout)
* Customer reviews (card slider style)
* CTA section (Reserve Now)

---

#### 2. Menu Page

* Category tabs:

  * Starters
  * Main Course
  * Cocktails
  * Mocktails
  * Desserts
* Menu cards:

  * Image (placeholder)
  * Dish name
  * Short description
* No prices
* Clean grid layout

---

#### 3. Gallery Page

* Sections:

  * Dining Area
  * Interior
  * Bar Area
  * Ambience
* Masonry/grid layout
* Image hover effects (zoom + overlay)

---

#### 4. Reservation Page

* Form UI:

  * Name
  * Phone
  * Date picker
  * Time slots (buttons)
  * Guests dropdown
* Show:

  * Available vs unavailable slots (disabled style)
* Submit button: “Book Table”

---

#### 5. About Page

* Story section
* Chef / team section
* Interior images
* Brand vibe

---

#### 6. Contact Page

* Address
* Phone number
* Opening hours
* Map placeholder
* Call-to-action button

---

### 🧩 Components to Create

* Navbar (sticky, transparent → solid on scroll)
* Footer (social icons, links)
* Buttons (primary, secondary, hover glow)
* Cards (menu, gallery, reviews)
* Form inputs (dark theme)
* Image placeholders (easy to replace)

---

### 🖼️ Image Handling (IMPORTANT)

* Use placeholder rectangles with labels:

  * “Replace Image”
* Maintain consistent aspect ratios
* Keep images as components for easy swapping

---

### ✨ Interactions (Prototype)

* Hover effects on buttons and cards
* Smooth transitions between pages
* Clickable navigation
* Active state for menu tabs

---

### 🧠 UX Focus

* Clean spacing and hierarchy
* Visual storytelling (ambience > text)
* Easy navigation
* Strong call-to-action for reservations

---

### 📦 Deliverables

* Organized Figma file
* Pages separated clearly
* Components reusable
* Auto layout used for responsiveness

Make the design visually rich, modern, and focused on showcasing ambience, cocktails, and dining experience rather than detailed food pricing.
